About Me

Hi, I'm Kira — a single mom of four, a student, and a fighter. I’ve faced rejection, judgment, and the uphill battle of rebuilding my life after a felony conviction five years ago. While the world continues to treat people like me as disposable, I’m working every day to prove otherwise.

I’m currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Information Technology with hopes of building a stable future for me and my children. I’ve applied to over 100 jobs and been rejected time after time, not because I’m not qualified — but because of my past. Still, I keep pushing forward.

I created this space to speak up — to share my truth, my frustration, and my resilience. I know I'm not alone. There are so many others out there navigating a broken system, trying to rise above it. This blog is for us.

If you've ever felt like the system was built to keep you down, I hope my story reminds you: your voice matters, your story matters, and you are not alone.
